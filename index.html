Our Mission

To nurture the next generation of Muslims through authentic Quranic education, Hifz, and Islamic values — producing not just students, but guardians of the Quran.

Our Vision

To become a leading center of Islamic learning in Rajshahi, providing free and affordable education to children from all backgrounds — especially the underprivileged.
